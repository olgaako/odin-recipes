Rice

Rice has to be perfect and fluffy

Rice out of the rice maker is always ideal.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup of Jasmine rice
  • 2 cups of water
  • rice maker

Steps

  1. Measure out 1 cup of rice and put into the rice maker's bowl.
  2. Rinse the rice in the bowl.
  3. Pour 2 cups of water into the rice bowl.
  4. Set the rice maker to the "white rice" function.
