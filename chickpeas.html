Tasty Chickpeas

My favorite way to prepare chickpeas

A chickpea snack that I learned on a road trip across America!

Ingredients

  • canned chickpeas
  • seasoning
  • honey
  • crunchy Taqis

Steps

  1. Open the chickpea can and drain the water out.
  2. Add the buffalo seasoning.
  3. Dabble some honey.
  4. Crush Taqis on top for extra crunch.
